About E-SUMMIT

The Entrepreneurship Summit

The E- Summit is the flagship event of E-Cell which is a platform for bringing together budding entrepreneurs, venture capitalists, investors, start-ups and other major contributors to the entrepreneurial ecosystem. This networking event gives a chance for people to interact and help build successful ventures. The summit also showcases the latest global trends in entrepreneurship.

  • Entrepreneurship is the process of designing, launching and running a new business, which is often initially a small business.
  • The Entrepreneurship Cell is dedicated to promoting the spirit of entrepreneurship among students throughout the college.
  • The club not only provides the budding entrepreneurs with a platform to convert their dreams into reality.
  • Being a specialisation in many B-Schools it is very important that entrepreneurship is promoted well and help students get clarity.

Vince Lombardi once said, “The price of success is hard work, dedication to the job at hand, and the determination that whether we win or lose, we have applied the best of ourselves to the task at hand.” We at IEDC of NMIMS Shirpur believe that the Indian youth have the dedication, capability, and energy to make India the abode of Creation, Inspiration and Investments. Thus we strive to provide a perfect platform for such edgy minds with (Disruptive / Innovative and Groundbreaking) ideas that showcase the potential to enable better lives, create better opportunities and prepare the grounds for a vivid developmental future. And we believe that the very first step towards success is to try and keep trying until you reach the horizons of your aura. So, In order to widen your vision and channelize the enthusiasm of the same uniqueness and “Parishram” in your young minds, we find immense privilege to Invite you all to our very own 1st E – Summit 2021 by IEDC, NMIMS Shirpur.

    • FAQ

    What is E-Summit?

    E summit otherwise known as entrepreneurship summit, is a meet or an event wherein the budding entrepreneurs of tommorow come together, share their ideas to the industrialists and business magnates and compete with their fellow entrepreneurs. There'll be a certain set of lucrative workshops too.

    How to reach Shirpur?

    Shirpur is a town tht lies in the district of Dhule, at the banksof river Tapi. It is safe to say that it is somewhere near the border of MP-Maharashtra border. It is well-connectedto other places by roadways, a 4 hour drive from Indore and an overnight 9 hour drive from Mumbai in a bus. One can also choose a private vehicle to travel for faster and uninterrupted transport. Our campus NMIMS Shirpur lies near the Mumbai-Agra highway. If you happen to catch a flight from your place itis advisable to drop off at Indore or Mumbai, and you can catch a cab or a bus from there on.

    Why should we attend an esummit?

    One should attend an E-Summit to showcase their entrepreneurship skills and open up their chances to get into the industry and give themselves a huge opportunity to flourish. Also they will get to interact with successful people in the industry and learn a lot about it, as to how it functions.
